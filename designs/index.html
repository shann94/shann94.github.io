My Designs




With graduation quickly approaching, I took it upon myself to design my own announcements to send to my family and friends. I designed this using Virginia Tech brand approved colors, fonts, and logos. I am very proud of my work and I am excited to share the graduation experience with my loved ones

To create this:

  • We pulled images from the Virginia Tech photo database
  • I used Adobe InDesign to create this design.




In the Fall of 2015, Katrina Powell was identified as a Distinguished Lecture Scholar for her book "Identity and Power in Narrative Displacements." After interviewing Dr. Powell, my group and I designed this poster to recognize her distinction. Dr. Powell asked for us to use the image in the poster. The image is from her book, and Dr. Powell wanted it to be the cover of the book, but the publisher thought otherwise. She believes that this image captures the overall feel of the book. When the design was completed, Dr. Powell was most than pleased with the design.

To create this:

  • We had to remaster the image to make it big enough for the post using Adobe Photoshop.
  • We used Adobe InDesign to create this design.
