During my last semester at Virginia Tech, I took a health communication course. In the course my two group members, Katharina Elberti and Megan Burpo, and I developed a public health campaign to boost awareness of the benefits of organ donation and to correct the myths that are commonly accepted called Part of Me: Part of You. Our target audience was Virginia Tech students. As part of the campaign, we developed a campaign website, a PSA (which can be see on the website), and table cards to disseminate information to the student body. The campaign is currently in the developmental phase and will be ready for viewing soon!
